Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Aksa's Booking system! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, protect, and share your personal information when you use our booking system and related services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Information: Email address, birth date, and role.
  • Booking Details: Appointment date, time, service type, and other relevant details.
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device information, and cookies to enhance user experience.
  • Communication Data: Messages and feedback provided through our contact forms or customer support channels.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To process and confirm your bookings.
  • To send appointment reminders and notifications.
  • To manage billing and payment processing.
  • To improve our services, website, and user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your information. However, we may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party providers who assist us with payment processing, email delivery, or technical support.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law, to comply with legal obligations or to protect our rights and interests.
  • Business Transfers: In case of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred to the new owner.

5. How We Protect Your Information

We implement robust security measures to protect your data:

  • Data encryption during transmission and storage.
  • Access controls and regular system audits.
  • Secure payment gateways for transactions.

Despite our efforts, no system is completely secure. We recommend you safeguard your account credentials.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent for certain data processing.
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority.

To exercise your rights, contact us at aksa.stranden@centria.fi.
